Synoptic matrix

Mark↔Matt · Matt↔Luke · Matt↔Luke masked · Mark↔Luke direct (18b)
Four Synoptic pairwise packages, read as one matrix.
The Synoptic problem has four pairwise evidence surfaces once the 2026-04-18b supplement is added: Mark↔Matthew (mm), Matt↔Luke full (ml), Matt↔Luke with likely-Markan verses masked out (mld), and — as of 18b — Mark↔Luke direct (mkl). This page is the cross-dataset view. The first section compares all four packages head-to-head; the second section keeps the Matt-centric triple-tradition triangle (what the old "triangle" page did); the third and fourth sections are scatter and verse-by-verse tables for the Matt-join. For the full Mark↔Luke pipeline, see the dedicated page 34.
