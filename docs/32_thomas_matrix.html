every Thomas logion against Matt / Mark / Luke / John

All 116 Thomas units (Coptic Scriptorium parse of NHC II,2) plotted against the four canonical gospels. Cells are populated from the curated Thomas parallel registry (47 entries); rows without registered parallels are shown too, to make visible that most of Thomas has no canonical convergence recorded in this workbench.

Why the scores are all zero in the automatic layer: the Thomas parse is Coptic and the canonical parse is Greek. Cross-lingual surface-string retrieval is not meaningful, so every thomas_to_canonical edge is flagged cross_lingual_no_auto_greek_score. The canonical-ref links you see in the matrix are curated semantic/sayings parallels with an assessed direct-dependence burden, not lexical matches. For one logion at a time, use 21 Thomas parallels; for the cross-corpus view, stay here.
