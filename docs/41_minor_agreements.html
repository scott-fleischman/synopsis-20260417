These two catalogs are the main data-driven discriminators between Q, Farrer, and Griesbach / Two-Gospel models at the system level. How to read: these are screening catalogs, not hand-curated critical lists. The minor-agreement strength is a combined metric (shared run + added/omitted tokens). The nonlocal_flag on a double-tradition row means Matthew and Luke place the same parallel at very different positions — that is the central displacement signal. Click a row to expand its Greek text.
