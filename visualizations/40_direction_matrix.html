Gospel direction matrix (4 × 4)

16 source → target cells · status · available burdens · best response models

This is the most compact way to see where every pairwise direction currently stands. Rows are the source (the text being used), columns are the target (the text that allegedly used it). Each off-diagonal cell names the direction, gives the atlas's current-status phrase, lists the available burden scores, and points to the best response model under that direction. Click a cell for a full panel including the core required explanations from the direction registry and deep-links into the per-direction dossier (page 37) and the support/contra card (below).

How to read this matrix: the status phrase is the atlas's qualitative conclusion, not a p-value. Available burden scores come from the ledgers in 50_direction_burden_reference.csv: direct_use and anchor_direct_use scales are not comparable across rows (direct-use scores are at the pericope/block level; anchor-direct-use scores are at the John anchor level). Self-identity cells on the diagonal are not applicable.
