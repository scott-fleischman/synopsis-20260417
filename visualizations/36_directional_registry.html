Directional hypothesis registry

twelve Synoptic+John direction hypotheses · current ranked conclusions · burden scores

A direction-by-direction catalog of the explanatory obligations each proposed dependence direction must carry. Every direction is represented — including the reverses (Mark used Matthew, Mark used Luke, Matthew used Luke, Synoptics used John) — because the question "what must this direction explain?" is only well-posed when the alternative direction is equally visible. Burden scores are audit prompts, not probabilities; they make explanatory obligations comparable, not probabilistically rank-ordered.
