10 ranked epistolary-gospel parallels · full alignment · supporting & contrary evidence

A dossier view of every high-value epistle-gospel parallel examined in this workbench. Each case is scored against three confidence axes (retrieval, philological, formula risk), then given a supporting and contrary evidence panel so the reader can see the interpretive alternatives that a best-explanation claim implicitly picks among.

The ranked list is sorted by strongest direct-dependence signal, not by confidence that the dependence is direct. A case with a long exact Greek run but heavy formulaic/liturgical risk (e.g., 1Cor 11:24 ~ Luke 22:19) can rank above a case whose best explanation favors direct dependence but with shorter overlap. The best-explanation box is where the burden model takes the signal seriously.
