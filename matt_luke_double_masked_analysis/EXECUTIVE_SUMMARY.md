# Matthew-Luke masked double-tradition executive summary

This rerun removes likely Markan material and rebuilds the Matthew-Luke candidate matrix, primary monotonic chain, secondary echoes, order blocks, pair diffs, variant notes, and directional burden ledger on the remaining material.

Main counts:

- Matthew verses masked as Markan: 389 of 1068; remaining unmasked: 679.
- Luke verses masked as Markan: 226 of 1149; remaining unmasked: 923.
- Filtered matrix: 626717 Matthew-Luke verse-pair rows.
- Primary monotonic chain: 49 pairs.
- Loose blocks: 13; tight blocks: 22.
- Secondary echoes retained: 60.

The package is intended to isolate the Matthew-Luke double-tradition problem after removing likely Markan material. It is most useful for comparing the explanatory burden of Luke-use-Matthew, Matthew-use-Luke, and common-source/oral models at the block and pair levels.

Inspect these files first: `data/01_markan_mask_by_verse.csv`, `data/07_primary_chain_pairs.csv`, `data/12_secondary_echoes.yaml`, `data/14_direction_ledger_by_loose_block.yaml`, and `data/19_pair_diffs_flat.tsv`.
